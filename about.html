Love For Brunch

We love brunch and we know that you do too. The Benedict was created on the idea to bring together people who love breakfast. Founded in 2015, The Benedict focused on creating nutritious recipes that are wholesome, comforting, and simply embodies more than just the typical avocado toast- although you will still find it on the menu! Located in La Jolla, California, come vist The Benedict and enjoy our catered menu items that will be sure to bring you back next time.
